ABOUT

The Dwelling of Dwarf is an interactive art piece about distance. The portable lanterns inhabited by dwarfs turn darker as people approach and brighter as they leave. In contrast to ordinary lighting fixtures, this mysterious behavior is an opportunity for visitors to think about the sense of distance between them and other people, as well as the sense of distance in the viewing experience.

When you look closely at art, you usually get close to it. But what if there is art that you can't see unless you move away from it? The sight of several people in a small room, stuck to a wall to watch, that in itself sounds interesting...! The Dwelling of Dwarf is a project launched in the summer of 2019 as a result of these fantasies. During its production, we plunged into the Covid-19 pandemic, and it made me think again about the meaning of the work.

STORY

There is a dwarf living in this lantern. This dwarf has the power to light the lights. In search of a safe place to live, the dwarf wanders from one light fixture to another after it has done its job and settles in the one he likes. Right now, this lantern seems to be his favorite.

But their lives are intertwined with a sense of urgency, not knowing when they will find a place to live. They live in secret so that no one can find them.

If a person approaches the lantern, the dwarf will be startled and disappear. Try not to scare them and watch them gently.

The habits of a dwarf

1

I prefer stay in quiet, small and compact places.

In a place that's too big, I don't know what to do.

2

If I stay long enough, I start to work on my house.

My current house has a handle on the entrance and tatami mats.

3

There's a network of dwarfs.

We share information about safe neighborhoods and new properties.
